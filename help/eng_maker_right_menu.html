The Right Menu

This menu give you the tools to customise every part.

After you connect your twitter account, you'll find a button to save the parts, so you could use them on the main page. In any case, the other buttons are:
  • Change the background image.
  • The brush tool (or press "b" on your keyboard).
  • The ereaser tool (or press "e" on your keyboard)
  • The color picker!
